Facilities for Students

1. In-house Infirmary
An in-house infirmary is located within our school where basic medical care and first aid is provided to all the students who are injured or become ill while on the premises. These facilities are staffed by medical professionals such as nurses; they are equipped with basic medical supplies and equipment to handle common medical issues and emergencies.

2. Ramps for CWSN students
Ramps for children with special needs (CWSN) are essential accessibility features in our school to ensure that students with mobility impairments can navigate the school environment safely and independently. Ramps play a crucial role in creating an inclusive and accessible learning environment for students with special needs, enabling them to fully participate in educational opportunities and reach their full potential.

3. Number of emergency exits
Our school have15 Emergency exits which are designated routes or doors in buildings that provide a safe means of escape during emergencies such as fires, earthquakes, or other situations requiring rapid evacuation. Emergency exits play a crucial role in building safety and are designed to facilitate swift and orderly evacuations, minimizing the risk of injury or loss of life during emergencies.

4. Safety Net
Our school building has a safety net from the roof to the railing in schools that can serve as an additional safety measure to prevent accidents and injuries, particularly in areas where there is a risk of falls from heights, such as balconies, atriums, or staircases.

5. Security Cameras
We have 100 plus cameras placed around and within our school campus which work 24x7 in classrooms and corridors contributing to creating a safer and more secure learning environment, promoting positive behavior, deterring misconduct, and facilitating effective responses to emergencies or incidents. Surveillance systems thoughtfully and transparently, with consideration for privacy concerns and in compliance with relevant laws and regulations.

6. Cafeteria
Our school cafeteria plays a multifaceted role in the educational experience of students, encompassing nutrition, socialization, education, safety, and inclusivity. It creates a cafeteria environment that prioritizes health, diversity, and sustainability contributes to the overall well-being and academic success of students.

7. AC buses
All our school buses have working air conditioners installed for a better and pleasant travel experience for students. The benefits in terms of student comfort, safety, and well-being.

8. Cognospace lab

Cognospace is an experiential learning platform that prioritizes activity based form of teaching. Students learn the entire spectrum of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) by performing hands-on activities by performing over 400+ activities Cognospace program is truly multidisciplinary as it covers textbook concepts in the form of activities as well as multiple engineering domains like Electrical Science, Structural Engg, Mechanics, Robotics and Automation and so on.This program of around 400+ activities is delivered by building a state-of-the-art laboratory that is powered by technology and a lot of other educational equipment. Technologies like Virtual Reality and 3D printing are used to expose kids to emerging technology domains.

9. Labs (Science/Math/Computer/Language)
School laboratories play a crucial role in enhancing students' learning experiences across various subjects.School laboratories in science, math, computer, and language disciplines serve as dynamic learning spaces that complement classroom instruction, promote hands-on exploration, and facilitate skill development in various subject areas. By providing opportunities for experiential learning, collaboration, and problem-solving. We have developed various such labs for the live learning of our students.
